We bring your ideas to life

We are a one-stop-shop for Circular Economy Excellence in Africa, inspiring and enabling the transition to a sustainable world. Our mission is to be an active part of fast-tracking the development towards a more circular and sustainable society, by creating a collaborative platform where stakeholders can grow, meet, influence and be seen.

What we offer

We create a platform where innovators, entrepreneurs, corporates, academia and policy makers can co-create circular solutions in an environment of trust and experimentation to address climate change, bio diversity loss and pollution

Accelerator Program

Our accelerator program is designed to walk with you from idea generation to scale up, connecting you with the right mentors, partners, opportunities and investors to see your idea come to life.
